Review A Site

Thrillist

  • What is The Company/Organization?
    The company is Thrillist and they are a media website that produces news, videos and a mix of several articles about various subjects.
  • What is the URL?
    Thrillist
  • What is the purpose of the site?
    The purpose of the site is to be a media outlet that provides the latest current events as well as articles and videos about travel, food and various other subjects.
  • Who is the intended audience?
    The taregeted audience are adults between the ages of 21 to about 40. Given the content it is defintely intended for adults and not for children.
  • Do you believe the site reaches its audience?
    I believe very much that it does. I know several people who frequent the site as well as their social medias and they are all in the same age range.
  • Was thr site useful to you? Why or Why not?
    This site is very useful to me because I love being able to keep up with current events and also be entertained by various videos or articles all in one place.
  • List the colors and/or graphics that are used on the home page of this website
    The home page is very eye catching they use rgb colors. Lots of red,black and white with very saturated jpg photos.
